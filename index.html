    CES 2020

    Who: SOURCE Global, PBC partnership with SBC

    What: A 160 sq. ft. product activation pop-up / tiny home

    Where: Las Vegas, NV

    Why: To promote award-winning sustainability technology, SOURCEÂ® Hydropanels, and to demonstrate our grid independent tiny home that was seen by over 10,000 visitors. These people tasted the water that was created using the Hydropanels, which uses the power of the sun to extract clean, pollutant-free drinking water from the air. Collected water is then mineralized for ideal composition and taste, making premium-quality drinking water a readily available resource.

    Why: Demonstrate Rapid build, sustainable building for emergency and homeless housing.
